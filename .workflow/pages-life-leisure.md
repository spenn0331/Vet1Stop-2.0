# Life & Leisure Page Blueprint

## Purpose & Goals
The Life & Leisure page serves as a comprehensive resource hub focusing on veterans' quality of life, recreation, and overall wellbeing outside of healthcare and career contexts. Its primary goals are to:

1. Connect veterans with recreational programs designed specifically for their needs
2. Provide information about veteran discounts, perks, and special opportunities
3. Guide veterans to resources that enhance their daily life and community connection
4. Offer adaptive recreation options for veterans with disabilities
5. Support veterans' holistic wellbeing through a variety of lifestyle resources

## Target User Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Recreation Seeker
A veteran looking for recreational activities, sports, or hobby groups that connect them with fellow veterans.

### Scenario 2: Disability Adaptation
A veteran with service-connected disabilities seeking adaptive recreation and lifestyle resources.

### Scenario 3: Family Activities
A veteran with a family seeking activities, events, and discounts that the whole family can enjoy.

### Scenario 4: Reintegration Support
A recently discharged veteran looking for community connections and activities to ease civilian transition.

## Interactive Elements

### Events Calendar
An interactive calendar showing veteran-focused events, activities, and programs searchable by location and category.

### Discount Finder
Searchable database of veteran discounts and perks filtered by category, location, and eligibility requirements.

### Activity Matcher
Quiz-style tool that suggests recreational activities based on interests, abilities, and location.

### Community Forum
Moderated space for veterans to connect about shared interests, organize meetups, and discuss leisure activities.

## Technical Considerations

### Data Sources
- VA recreation program directory
- Veteran service organization activities database
- National and state park information
- Aggregated discount programs for veterans
- User-generated event listings (with moderation)

### Search Functionality
- Location-based searches for nearby activities
- Filtering by activity type, accessibility needs, and cost
- Seasonal and date-based filtering for events
- Family-friendly filter options

### Personalization
- Saved favorite activities and programs
- Interest-based recommendations
- Personalized discount suggestions
- Custom event notifications

### Accessibility Features
- Detailed accessibility information for all recreation venues
- Clear indicators for adaptive programs
- Screen reader optimized content
- Alternative text for activity images
- Section labels and ARIA attributes
- Keyboard navigable calendar and search interfaces

## Success Metrics
- Activity and program link click-through rates
- Event calendar usage and engagement
- Discount finder utilization
- Return visits to Life & Leisure section
- User-generated content contributions
- Social sharing of activities and events
- User feedback on resource quality

## Content Refresh Strategy
- Weekly updates to event calendar
- Monthly addition of new recreational resources
- Quarterly verification of discount programs
- Seasonal featured activities rotation
- Annual comprehensive review of all listings

This blueprint provides a comprehensive framework for building a Life & Leisure page that enriches veterans' lives through recreation, community connection, and quality of life enhancements, with special attention to adaptive needs and family inclusion.
