# Vet1Stop Project Overview

**Mission:** Vet1Stop is the premier centralized hub for U.S. veterans — connecting them with resources (Health, Education, Careers, Life & Leisure), community (Social), and veteran-owned businesses (Local, Shop).

**Founded by** a service-disabled veteran entrepreneur. SDVOSB-eligible.
