# Local Page Blueprint

## Purpose & Goals
The Local page serves as a discovery platform for veteran-owned businesses and veteran-friendly establishments in users' local communities. Its primary goals are to:

1. Highlight and promote veteran-owned businesses to increase their visibility and patronage
2. Connect consumers with local businesses that offer veteran discounts and support
3. Provide veterans with a platform to showcase their businesses to a targeted audience
4. Create an ecosystem that economically empowers the veteran community
5. Build awareness of the significant contributions veterans make to local economies

## Target User Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Consumer Supporting Veteran Businesses
A consumer (veteran or non-veteran) who specifically wants to patronize and support veteran-owned businesses in their area.

### Scenario 2: Veteran Business Owner
A veteran who owns a business and wants to list it on the platform to increase visibility to targeted customers.

### Scenario 3: Veteran Seeking Discounts
A veteran looking for local businesses that offer special discounts or services to veterans.

### Scenario 4: Relocating Veteran
A veteran moving to a new area who wants to find veteran-friendly establishments and build connections.

## Content Structure

### Hero Section
- Engaging hero image showcasing diverse veteran-owned businesses
- Headline: "Support Local Veteran Businesses"
- Brief introduction emphasizing the impact of supporting veteran entrepreneurs
- Primary CTA: "Find Veteran Businesses Near You"
- Secondary CTA: "List Your Veteran-Owned Business"

### Map-Based Business Finder
- Interactive map interface similar to Google Maps
- Business pins color-coded by category
- List view alternative for accessibility
- Filter controls prominently displayed
- Current location detection with permission
- Search bar for location or business type
- Recently viewed businesses section

### Business Categories
- Restaurants & Food Services
- Retail & Shopping
- Professional Services
- Trades & Construction
- Health & Wellness
- Technology & Digital Services
- Arts & Entertainment
- Automotive
- Home Services
- Each category includes featured businesses and quick search functionality

### Business Listings
- Comprehensive profiles for each business including:
  - High-quality photos
  - Description and veteran story
  - Contact information and hours
  - Services/products offered
  - Veteran status (Veteran-owned, Service-disabled veteran-owned)
  - Special offers for veterans
  - Reviews and ratings
  - Social media links
  - Directions and map integration

### Special Offers Section
- Highlighted businesses with current veteran discounts
- Limited-time promotions
- Loyalty programs for veterans
- Community support initiatives
- Featured veteran discount programs

### Business Registration Portal
- Simple submission form for veteran business owners
- Verification process explanation
- Benefits of listing on the platform
- Enhancement options (featured listings, promotion packages)
- Success stories from listed businesses
- Analytics dashboard preview

### Community Impact
- Statistics on veteran-owned businesses in the economy
- Success stories and business spotlights
- Economic impact visualizations
- Community reinvestment initiatives
- Veteran business grant recipients

### Resource Finder Tool
- Interactive tool to search for:
  - Business support resources by location
  - Local chambers of commerce with veteran programs
  - Networking events for veteran business owners
  - Local mentor connections
  - Search filters for resource type and location

## Interactive Elements

### Business Search & Filter
Comprehensive search functionality with filters for:
- Business category
- Distance from location
- Veteran-owned or veteran-friendly
- Special offers available
- Accessibility features
- Online ordering/services available
- Operating hours

### Review & Rating System
User-generated reviews with verification for authentic customer experiences and special "Veteran Verified" badges for reviews by veterans.

### Business Claim Process
System for veteran business owners to claim existing listings or add new businesses with verification steps.

### Favorites & Lists
Ability for users to save favorite businesses, create custom lists (like "Weekend Favorites" or "Service Providers"), and share lists with others.

### Special Offer Alerts
Opt-in notifications for new veteran discounts or special offers from saved businesses or within specified categories.

## Integration with Other Pages

### Entrepreneur Page Integration
- Resources for local business owners
- Connection between entrepreneurship resources and business listing opportunities
- Local success stories featured in both sections

### Shop Page Integration
- Local businesses that also sell products online
- Pickup options for online purchases
- Cross-promotion between local physical stores and online shop listings

### Social Page Integration
- Local business networking events
- Community gatherings at veteran-owned establishments
- Business owner meet-ups and support groups

## Technical Considerations

### Data Sources
- Veteran business certification databases
- User-submitted business listings (with verification)
- Google Maps API or similar mapping service
- Geocoding services for location data
- Image storage and CDN for business photos

### Search Functionality
- Geographic proximity search (find businesses within X miles)
- Category and sub-category filtering
- Keyword search across business descriptions
- Filter for currently open businesses
- Special offer and discount filtering

### Personalization
- Location-based recommendations
- Previously visited businesses
- Saved favorite businesses
- Category preferences
- Custom alerts for new businesses meeting criteria

### Accessibility Features
- Alternative to map-based interface
- Screen reader compatible business listings
- Location services alternatives
- Clear text descriptions of all business images
- Keyboard navigable search interface
- Location information in multiple formats

## Success Metrics
- Business listing search frequency
- Profile view-to-visit conversion rates
- New business registrations
- Review submission rates
- Return visitor frequency
- Click-through on directions and contact information
- Special offer redemptions (if trackable)

## Content Refresh Strategy
- Daily updates to special offers
- Weekly addition of new business listings
- Monthly featured business rotation
- Quarterly community impact updates
- Annual verification of all business information

This blueprint provides a comprehensive framework for building a Local page that connects consumers with veteran-owned and veteran-friendly businesses, creating economic opportunities for veterans while allowing the broader community to support their services.
