# Careers & Entrepreneurship Page Blueprint

## Purpose & Goals
The Careers page serves as a comprehensive hub for veterans pursuing professional opportunities, whether through traditional employment or entrepreneurship. Its primary goals are to:

1. Help veterans translate their military skills to civilian workforce equivalents or business ventures
2. Connect veterans with employers actively seeking to hire military talent
3. Provide guidance on career development, from resume building to business planning
4. Offer resources for employment opportunities and business funding
5. Support veterans at every stage of their professional journey, whether as employees or entrepreneurs

## Target User Scenarios

### Traditional Employment Scenarios

#### Scenario 1: Transitioning Service Member
A service member preparing to leave active duty who needs to understand civilian job markets and translate their military experience.

#### Scenario 2: Employed Veteran Seeking Advancement
A veteran already in the civilian workforce looking to advance their career or gain additional skills.

#### Scenario 3: Veteran Facing Employment Challenges
A veteran who has been unemployed or underemployed and needs specialized employment assistance.

#### Scenario 4: Disabled Veteran
A veteran with service-connected disabilities seeking accommodating employment options or vocational rehabilitation.

### Entrepreneurship Scenarios

#### Scenario 5: Aspiring Entrepreneur
A veteran with a business idea who needs guidance on how to get started, create a business plan, and secure initial funding.

#### Scenario 6: Early-Stage Business Owner
A veteran who has recently launched a business and needs support with growth, marketing, and operational challenges.

#### Scenario 7: Established Business Seeking Government Contracts
A veteran with an established business looking to get certified as a Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) or Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) to access government contracting opportunities.

#### Scenario 8: Scaling Business
A veteran with a successful business looking to scale operations, secure larger funding, or expand into new markets.
